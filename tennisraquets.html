Most important factors to consider when purchasing a tennis racquet

  • Weight:Lighter racquets are easier to swing and control, which can help beginners generate power.
  • Grip:The right grip size is important for comfort and handling.
  • Head Size:Beginners may benefit from a larger head size, which is more forgiving if they don't hit the ball in the center.
  • Length:Longer racquets can offer more balance, power, and stability.
